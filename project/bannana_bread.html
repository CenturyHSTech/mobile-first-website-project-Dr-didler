Recipe for Bannana Bread

Prep

*Dont forget to get ripe bananas

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F, then Grease an 8.5 by 4.5 inch loaf pan, Take out a mit to set the dish on after the oven

Recipe:

use double the banana requested!!

  1. 1 and 1/3 cup flour, 3/4 teaspoon salt,1/2 teaspoon baking soda,1/4 teaspoon baking powder,mix the previous four ingredients,then mix the following: 5 and 1/3 tbsp butter, 2/3 cup sugar,mix these in after everything els has been mixed. Mix in two large eggs,2 cups mushed bannana, mix in half a cup of chocolate chips, and throw in oven for 55 min

history of bannana bread

Banana bread became popular in the United States during the 1930s, when baking soda and baking powder were becoming common in home kitchens. As bananas ripened quickly, home cooks began using them in quick breads instead of throwing them away.

The first widely recognized banana bread recipes appeared in community cookbooks and commercial pamphlets, especially those published by flour and baking powder companies. Its sweet flavor, moist texture, and simple preparation helped it become a household favorite.

By the mid-20th century, banana bread had become a classic comfort food and a staple for using up overripe bananas. Today, it remains a beloved homemade treat, often adapted with add-ins like nuts, chocolate chips, or spices.
